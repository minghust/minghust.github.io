Brief Biography: Ming Zhang is a Ph.D. student in Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) advised by Prof. Yu Hua. He obtained the B.E. degree in Computer Science from HUST in 2019.

His current research interests include persistent memory system and architecture, distributed and disaggregated system, and memory security.
