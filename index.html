Brief Biography: Ming Zhang is a Ph.D. student in Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) advised by Prof. Yu Hua. He obtained the B.E. degree in Computer Science from HUST in 2019. He has published papers in major conferences and journals in the areas of computer systems and architectures. His current research interests include distributed in-memory systems, memory disaggregation, and persistent memory.
