Ming Zhang（张铭）

Ph.D student in Computer System Architecture
Huazhong University of Science and Technology (Enrollment Certification)


Biography: Ming Zhang is a Ph.D student in Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) advised by Prof. Yu Hua. He obtained the B.E degree in Computer Science from HUST in 2019.

He currently focuses on researching computer systems and architecture, including persistent memory, distributed system, and memory security.
